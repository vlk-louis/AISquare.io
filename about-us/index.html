Our Journey

The story of the power of 2

International by Default

Twelve years ago, our journey began in Montreal, where we embarked on an exhilarating startup adventure. The years that followed saw us take diverging paths, with Nicolas venturing to Silicon Valley and Louis making Singapore his base of operations. However, our friendship and shared passion for innovation endured.

Our global perspectives converged in 2022 when the stars aligned, and we decided to breathe life into our common vision. Today, we're here as the co-founders of AI Square, a company that embodies the synergy of two distinct worlds – Silicon Valley's cutting-edge technology and Asia's dynamic industrial landscape.

Our mission is clear, to leverage the power of AI to tackle pivotal industrial challenges across sectors that form the backbone of our economy. Whether it's in healthcare, logistics, infrastructure and beyond.
AI Square Corp. is headquarted in San Francisco with a regional base in Singapore.

Louis Boka | CEO

A global citizen with a third-culture upbringing, driven by a quest to connect and understand. From a young age, he was captivated by video game worlds. His passion evolved into a fascination with the potential of design, propelling him into the world of computer graphics during university.

Stepping into the professional realm, Louis embraced technology's power to solve complex business challenges and embarked on a career marked by diverse successes, including critical roles in four startups that achieved acquisition. Notably, he contributed to SquareClock's integration with Dassault Systemes, and UrbanFox's alignment with GEODIS. These experiences honed his expertise.

Since his humble beginings as a front-end developer at Societe Generale, Louis's journey expanded through Accenture, refining his business acumen.

His deep industry roots enable him to build connections and drive AI solutions delivering impact globally. Recognized with the Innovation Award for AI and IoT by BPI France, Louis is a deterministic optimist, relentless in pushing boundaries.

Nicolas Duval | CTO

With a robust career spanning over 16 years, Nicolas stands out as a beacon in the realms of software architecture, design, development, and management.

His profound expertise in cloud computing, rapid prototyping, computer-aided design, Machine Learning, generative AI, and application development have paved the way for innovative breakthroughs in the field of software engineering.

Before founding AI Square, Nicolas worked at Adobe as a software architect where he played a pivotal role in the conception and execution of the Adobe next-generation platform, Adobe Express.

Before joining Adobe, Nicolas was the principal engineer at Autodesk, and championed the transformation of both Autodesk Forge platform and Tinkercad.
