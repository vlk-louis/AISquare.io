Infrastructure

In the realm of infrastructure and IoT, where hardware reigns supreme, the promise of predictive maintenance and cost efficiency is no longer a futuristic concept. We delve into the heart of this transformative technology that we are creating in collaboration with major ports in Asia.

Enhanced Predictive Maintenance (Hardware Lifecycle Analysis):

Along with predicting when a component might fail, the system continuously monitors the lifecycle of the hardware. It keeps a track record of when devices start to degrade in performance versus compared to its optimal specs.

Over time, if system notices that certain devicess degrade faster than average in a particular environment or setup, it flags them for recommendation. This way resource allocation can be focused on checking devices that are likely to fail instead of everything.

Hardware Recommendation Engine:

Based on the data collected over time, the system can make informed suggestions on hardware replacements or upgrades.

Recommendations are not just based on performance degradation but also on factors like energy efficiency, compatibility with other devices, and even feedback from similar setups elsewhere.

Whenever the system recognizes a pattern or trend where a piece of devices repeatedly underperforms in a specific scenario, it starts suggesting alternatives which have shown better resilience or efficiency in similar conditions.

Integration with Suppliers:

The system can be integrated with databases from hardware suppliers. When it makes a recommendation, it also provides real-time price quotes or even place orders for replacements.

The collaboration with suppliers, generates commissions for AI Square and volume discounts for the client organisation.

Client Dashboard & Notifications:

A user-friendly dashboard for clients to view the health of their devices, the savings they’ve made through energy optimization, and devices upgrade/replacement suggestions.

Clients receive notifications when an upgrade can result in significant savings or when a piece of devices repeatedly underperforms.

Monetization Strategy:

Subscription for the platform:

A SaaS model where clients pay a recurring fee for the software platform.

Commission on Hardware Sales:

Whenever the client accepts a recommendation and makes a purchase, AI Square gets a commission.

Bulk Deals and Partnerships:

AI Square can strike deals with devices suppliers. When we notice a pattern across multiple clients needing a particular upgrade, we can negotiate bulk purchase rates.
