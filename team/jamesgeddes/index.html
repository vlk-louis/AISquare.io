Meet James Geddes: Pioneering Engineer Driving Innovation at AI Square

James Geddes is the Head of Engineering at AI Square, where he leverages his extensive expertise in software development, blockchain technology, and AI to propel the company’s groundbreaking solutions forward. With a career marked by innovation and excellence, James is a driving force behind AI Square’s mission to revolutionize the future of work, education, and communication.

A Journey Through Technological Innovation

James’s journey began at the University of Minnesota, where he played a pivotal role in advanced research on neutrino detection. As a junior and assistant scientist, he led teams in the construction and verification of PVC electron neutrino detectors, showcasing his early leadership and technical skills.

In 2014, James transitioned to Seagate Technology as a Systems Integration Engineer. Here, he was instrumental in integrating new firmware features for Enterprise SSDs, developing rigorous tests in C and C++, and automating processes with Python. His contributions significantly enhanced the performance and stability of Seagate’s SSDs, demonstrating his ability to drive innovation in complex technical environments.

James’s foray into blockchain technology began at Animoca Brands, where he spent over six years working on blockchain integration, game programming, and decentralized applications (DApps). His work on Ethereum-based applications and non-fungible tokens (NFTs) positioned Animoca Brands at the cutting edge of the blockchain gaming industry.

In 2022, James joined Red Door Digital as a Blockchain Developer, focusing on Ethereum, smart contracts, and NFTs. His innovative solutions in decentralized applications paved the way for his role at We3D Design Studio, where he served as a Blockchain and Full-stack Developer. At We3D, James developed decentralized applications and smart contracts, further honing his expertise in blockchain technology and full-stack development.

Leading Engineering Excellence at AI Square

Since joining AI Square in March 2024, James has been at the helm of engineering excellence as the Head of Engineering. He leads the team in developing HyperSpeak, AI Square’s flagship product designed to transform meetings, education, and collaboration. By utilizing AI-driven parallel conversations and time travel, HyperSpeak reduces meeting times by 75%, enabling teams to maximize efficiency and productivity.

Under James’s leadership, the engineering team at AI Square is creating innovative solutions that redefine communication and collaboration in the modern workplace. His technical acumen and visionary approach are pivotal in driving AI Square’s mission to harness the power of AI for real-world impact.

A Visionary Technologist

James’s technical expertise spans blockchain development, full-stack development, continuous integration and delivery (CI/CD), cloud platforms, and game programming. His proficiency in these areas allows him to lead AI Square’s engineering team in developing cutting-edge AI solutions that address the most pressing industrial challenges.

At AI Square, James is not just an engineer; he is a visionary technologist committed to pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. His dedication to innovation and excellence ensures that AI Square remains at the forefront of AI-driven transformation.

Looking Ahead

James Geddes continues to lead AI Square’s engineering team with a vision of integrating advanced technologies to solve real-world problems. His relentless pursuit of innovation and excellence drives AI Square’s mission forward, setting new standards for the future of work, education, and communication.
