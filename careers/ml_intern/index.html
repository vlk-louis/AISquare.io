Machine Learning Intern

Company Overview

At AI SQUARE, we’re at the intersection of machine learning’s vast potential and the limitless world of computer graphics. Pioneering and redefining boundaries, our goal is to produce immersive experiences that synthesize imagination with state-of-the-art technology.

Position

Machine Learning Intern

Location

San Francisco or remote

Role Overview

As our Machine Learning Intern, you will be an essential part of a pioneering team, exploring the confluence of machine learning algorithms and dynamic visual content. Under the mentorship of seasoned professionals, you’ll contribute to translating visionary ideas into real-world applications that redefine what’s possible.

Key Responsibilities

  • Explore and Contribute: Engage with machine learning algorithms to enhance the visual fidelity and interactive experiences of our projects.
  • Research and Learn: Stay updated with the evolving landscape of machine learning, participating in discussions and brainstorming sessions to keep our products at the cutting edge.
  • Implement and Test: Under guidance, translate abstract concepts into functional prototypes and products, ensuring the quality and effectiveness of visual representations.

Qualifications

  • Pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
  • Basic understanding of machine learning.
  • Eager to learn, with a knack for problem-solving and a drive to delve into new technological territories.
  • Familiarity with frameworks like PyTorch, Tensorflow is desirable.
  • Introductory knowledge of computer graphics technologies like OpenGL is a plus.

What We Offer

  • A platform to learn, innovate, and contribute towards blending machine learning with visual storytelling.
  • Competitive internship stipend.
  • Flexible working hours and the choice between remote or on-site work settings.

To Apply

Please send us your resume, along with a cover letter expressing your motivation for this role and any relevant coursework or projects. If available, a portfolio showcasing your initial journey in machine learning and computer graphics would be appreciated.
